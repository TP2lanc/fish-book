Brownie & Bo at the Big Wood Diner is a hardcover illustrated children's book that takes a look at two hungry trout as they spin a tale of their life in the rivers and lakes of the West.

Stunning watercolor illustrations flow from page to
page as Brownie and Bo swim you through their life…

From encounters with fisher-people, who are really quite sly…

or learning to eat the right type of flies…

see the beauty of the American west through their fishy eyes.

About the Author

Lynn W. Peters fell in love with Idaho on her first visit during the winter of 1998 when she discovered Nordic skiing on the Harriman Trail. She has spent increasing amounts of time in the state, in all seasons, every year since. When back East, she splits her time between Lancaster, PA and Maryland’s Eastern Shore

About the Illustrator

Zack D. Brown is an illustrator and graphic designer who has been privileged to call Hailey, Idaho, home for the better part of his life. He studied graphic design and illustration at John Brown University. Zack now resides in Orlando, Florida, where he works as a digital designer.
